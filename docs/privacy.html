JustRoll — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

JustRoll shares the photos you take while hanging out with the friends who were there with you. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles, why, and what control you have over it. Short version: we collect the minimum needed to deliver your photos to your circle, we never sell data, and we don't track you.

What we collect

  • Account details — your email address, display name, and username, so friends can find you and circles know who's who.
  • Photos and videos you choose to send — when you end a roll, the app shows you every shot from that time window and you pick what to share. Only the shots you approve are uploaded. Nothing leaves your phone silently.
  • Circle activity — when you join and leave a roll (timestamps), which determines who receives which photos.
  • A push notification token — so your phone can be told when photos or friends arrive.

What we don't do

  • No tracking, no advertising identifiers, no analytics SDKs.
  • No selling or sharing data with third parties for their own use.
  • No scanning of your photo library beyond the time window of rolls you started, and never without the photo permission you grant.

Photo library access

JustRoll asks for photo library access so it can find the photos whose capture time falls inside your roll's start and end times. That's the entire use: a time-window filter. You review and deselect anything before it's sent, and received photos are saved back to your camera roll.

Where your data lives

Data is stored with Supabase (our backend provider), protected by row-level security so photos are only readable by the members of the circle they were shared with. Photos are transferred over HTTPS.

Retention and deletion

  • Uploaded photos are deleted from our servers automatically once every recipient has received (or dismissed) them — the server is a relay, not an album.
  • You can delete your account any time in Settings → Delete account. This immediately and permanently erases your profile, your unsent and undelivered photos, your circle memberships, and your contacts. It cannot be undone.
  • You can also email us (address below) to request deletion.

Your consent

Photos are only ever shared after you explicitly review and approve them. You can withdraw consent at any time by not sending, discarding an unsent roll, leaving a circle, or deleting your account.

Children

JustRoll is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above changes with it, and material changes will be called out in the app.
